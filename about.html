As a passionate programmer, I strive to constantly learn and improve myself

I come from Singapore, and am currently studying in Christchurch, New Zealand, towards my Bachelor's Degree in Science, major in Computer Science.

I was first introduced to the world of programming by stumbling on to Python from a casual remark by my friend. Thereafter, I went to find out more about Python on Codecademy and the interest eventually developed into passion.

Now, I am an aspiring programmer who strives to constantly learn and improve myself. As I first picked up Python, I was naturally exposed to Algorithms and Data Structure before everything else. It has since become one of my self-motivated goal to learn various concepts in this aspect; solving probelms on LeetCode as well as delving into Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.

Apart from that, being an avid gamer - League of Legends, Pokemon, Terraria, Stardew Valley and so on - I become inclined towards not just Game Development, but also Software Development, which was what motivated me to create this personal showcase of mine.
